Technical Information

How We Use Your Information

AI-Powered Career Services

To provide personalized job recommendations, resume analysis, career coaching, and skill gap identification.

Platform Improvement

To enhance our AI models, improve user experience, and develop new features based on usage patterns.

Communication

To send important updates, security notifications, and relevant career opportunities (with your consent).

Security & Compliance

To protect against fraud, ensure platform security, and comply with legal obligations.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We DO NOT Sell Your Data

LakshyaAI never sells, rents, or trades your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing Scenarios

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted partners who help us operate our platform (cloud hosting, analytics, customer support).
  • Job Applications: When you apply for jobs through our platform, we share your application materials with the respective employers.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.
  • Aggregated Data: We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics that don't identify individual users.

Data Security & Retention

Security Measures

Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security updates
  • Intrusion detection systems

Operational Security

  • Access controls and monitoring
  • Employee security training
  • Regular security audits
  • Incident response procedures

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active and for legitimate business purposes.
  • Inactive Accounts: Data from inactive accounts may be deleted after 3 years of inactivity.
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations.
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, non-identifiable data may be retained indefinitely for research and improvement.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and understand how you use our platform.

Essential Cookies

Required for basic functionality like login, security, and preferences. Cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

Preference Cookies

Remember your choices and personalize your experience. Can be managed individually.

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings or our preference center. Note that disabling essential cookies may limit platform functionality.
